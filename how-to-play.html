How to Play Puzzle

Objective

Create words on the board using letter tiles to score points. The player with the highest score wins!

Game Setup

Each player starts with 7 letter tiles. The board is a 15x15 grid with special premium squares that multiply your word or letter scores.

Taking Turns

On your turn, place tiles on the board to form a word. All words must connect to existing words on the board (except for the first word, which must cover the center star).

Scoring

  • Triple Word (TW): Triples the entire word score
  • Triple Letter (TL): Triples the letter score
  • Double Word (DW): Doubles the entire word score
  • Double Letter (DL): Doubles the letter score

Winning

The game ends when all tiles have been used and one player has no tiles left, or when no more moves are possible. The player with the highest score wins!
