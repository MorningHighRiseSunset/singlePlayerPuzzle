How to Play

1. Playing Tiles:

  • On desktop: Click and drag tiles from your rack to the board
  • On mobile: Touch and hold a tile for 1 second, then drag to place it
  • To remove a tile, drag it back to your rack

2. Creating Words:

  • Place tiles horizontally or vertically
  • First word must cross the center star
  • New words must connect to existing tiles
  • All words formed must be valid

3. Offline Mode:

  • This game works completely offline
  • Uses 657,803 word dictionary locally
  • No internet connection required
  • No API calls made
